FAQ & Policies 📜

How does WhatsApp Checkout work?

When you click "Send Order via WhatsApp", your cart details and delivery address are formatted into a secure message. We will review your order and reply with payment instructions. No payment is taken directly on the site.

Shipping Timelines

We use DTDC for reliable delivery. Please allow 2-4 days for crafting and processing before dispatch. You will receive a tracking link on WhatsApp once shipped.

Returns & Exchanges

Returns are accepted if the item arrives damaged or incorrectly sized. Please send us a photo on WhatsApp within 48 hours of delivery.
